## Ingredients
* 1 cup granulated sugar
* 3 tablespoons brown sugar
* ½ teaspoon salt
* 1 cup corn syrup (light or dark)
* ⅓ cup salted butter (melted)
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 3 eggs
* 1 cup chopped pecans

## Steps
1.  Prepare the [pastry](pastry-pie-crust) or [graham cracker](graham-cracker-pie-crust) crust, but do not bake the crust.
2.  Preheat the oven to 350°F.
3.  In a large bowl, mix the granulated sugar, brown sugar, salt, corn syrup, butter, vanilla, and eggs.
4.  Pour the pecans into the unbaked pie crust.
5.  Pour the syrup mixture over the pecans.
6.  Cover the pie with aluminum foil.
7.  Bake for 30 minutes.
8.  Remove the foil.
9.  Bake for approximately 20 minutes. Check the pie regularly to make sure the crust and pecans do not burn.
10. Check the filling. It should jiggle a little. If there is too much movement, cover with foil again and continue cooking checking regularly.
11. Allow pie to cool for several hours or overnight.
